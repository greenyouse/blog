About Me (in goofy 3rd person speak)

Ed is a chemist turned programmer that loves to work on tricky problems and hack in LISPs. In late 2011 he started R programming for a university course and enjoyed it so much that he dove into learning AppleScript, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in his free time. A few months later he happened across some infamous Paul Graham essays and has been hooked on Clojure (and other LISPs) ever since.

When not programming he likes to ski, cook, geek out on video games, and go rock climbing.
